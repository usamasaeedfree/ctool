About Ctool.fun

Your trusted hub for fast, accurate, and free online tools.

At Ctool.fun, we create powerful, easy-to-use online calculators, converters, and utilities for students, professionals, and everyday users. Whether you’re calculating your GPA, converting percentages, or optimizing your SEO, our tools are built for speed, accuracy, and simplicity — no signup required.


Our Mission

Our mission is simple — make accurate, reliable tools accessible to everyone for free. We believe that the right tools can save time, improve decision-making, and empower learning.


Our Story

How It All Started

Ctool.fun began as a small personal project to help students calculate GPA and CGPA according to their university policies. As more people started using our tools, we expanded into multiple categories — academic calculators, SEO tools, and general converters. Today, thousands of users trust us for quick and precise results every day.
