Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: What is the passing GPA in UOG?

At UOG, the minimum passing GPA is 2.00 (C grade), but students must also meet departmental requirements for specific programs.

Q2: How can I improve my GPA at UOG?

You can improve your GPA by retaking failed subjects, scoring higher in credit-heavy courses, and staying consistent across all semesters.

Q3: Does UOG round off GPA?

Yes, UOG rounds GPA to two decimal places for transcripts and official records.

Q4: What CGPA is required for degree completion at UOG?

For most programs, UOG requires a minimum 2.00 CGPA to qualify for graduation. Some departments may require higher.

Q5: Is this GPA calculator official?

This tool follows the official UOG grading policy but is developed by ctool.fun as a free academic resource. It is not an official UOG website.

Q6: How do I calculate UOG GPA manually?

Multiply grade points by credit hours, add them up, and divide by total credit hours. Our calculator automates this process instantly.
