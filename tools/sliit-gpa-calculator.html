Official SLIIT Grading System (Updated 2025)

The Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT) follows a 4.0-grade point system to evaluate student performance in undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The following table shows the official SLIIT GPA scale with corresponding grade points and marks range.

Understanding this scale helps students calculate their SLIIT GPA and CGPA accurately using our online GPA calculator tool. Each grade reflects a specific level of academic achievement and contributes proportionally to your overall GPA.

SLIIT Grading Scale and Grade Point System
Grade Grade Point Marks Range (%)
A+ 4.0 90 - 100
A 4.0 80 - 89
A- 3.7 75 - 79
B+ 3.3 70 - 74
B 3.0 65 - 69
B- 2.7 60 - 64
C+ 2.3 55 - 59
C 2.0 45 - 54
C- 1.7 40 - 44
D+ 1.3 35 - 39
D 1.0 30 - 34
E 0.0 0 - 29

The SLIIT grading system ensures fair academic evaluation across all courses and credit units. Students can use this table with the SLIIT GPA Calculator above to instantly find their semester or cumulative GPA. This transparent grading model helps learners track academic progress and plan for better results.

FAQ's

What is the SLIIT GPA Calculator?

The SLIIT GPA Calculator is an online academic tool that helps Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT) students calculate their GPA and CGPA accurately based on the official grading scale. Simply enter your grades and credits to get instant results.

How can I calculate my GPA for SLIIT online?

You can calculate your GPA online using our SLIIT GPA Calculator. Just select your grades, enter the number of credits for each subject, and click “Calculate.” The tool automatically computes your weighted GPA according to the SLIIT system.

What is the difference between SLIIT GPA and CGPA?

Your GPA reflects the performance in a single semester, while your CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) represents the overall average across all semesters. The SLIIT CGPA Calculator combines all your semester results to show your total academic standing.

Can I use this SLIIT Merit Calculator for admissions?

Yes, the SLIIT Merit Calculator helps estimate your merit ranking for admissions or scholarships by combining GPA, test results, and other criteria according to SLIIT guidelines. It gives you an idea of your eligibility before applying.

Can I calculate both GPA and CGPA with this tool?
