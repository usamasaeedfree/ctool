FAQ's for the VIT's Students

What is the U of T GPA Calculator?

The UofT GPA Calculator is a handy tool designed for University of Toronto students to quickly figure out their GPA, final course percentages, and estimated final grades using the official U of T grading scale.

How does the University of Toronto GPA Scale work?

The official U of T GPA Scale converts your percentage marks into grade points on a 4.0 scale. This system helps measure academic performance across different faculties.

What is the UofT Grading Scale?

The UofT Grading Scale includes letter grades from A+ to F. Each letter corresponds to a percentage range and GPA value used across all U of T campuses — St. George, UTM, and UTSC.

How can I use the U of T Grade Calculator?

Just enter your course grades or percentages, and the calculator will instantly display your GPA and overall average.

What does the U of T Final Grade Calculator do?

The U of T Final Grade Calculator helps estimate your final course mark. Enter assignment and exam weights to see the grade needed to reach your target GPA.

Is there a Final Grade Calculator for U of T students?

Yes! The Final Grade Calculator U of T tool lets students predict semester or final GPAs before official grades are released.

What is the U of T GPA Calculator for Engineering?

The U of T GPA Calculator Engineering version follows the same 4.0 system but aligns with the grading standards of the Faculty of Applied Science and Engineering.

Can I use the U of T GPA Calculator for UTM?

Yes, the U of T GPA Calculator UTM works perfectly for students at the University of Toronto Mississauga, using the same grading scale as other campuses.

What is the U of T GPA Calculator Scale?

The U of T GPA Calculator Scale defines how percentage marks convert into GPA values (from 0.0 to 4.0) under the university’s official grading system.

How can I convert U of T GPA to Percentage?

Use the U of T GPA to Percentage converter to translate your GPA into an equivalent percentage based on the university’s official formula.

How do I calculate GPA at U of T?

Multiply each course’s grade point by its credit weight, sum the totals, then divide by total credits — or use our online U of T Calculate GPA tool for quick results.

What are the U of T GPA Requirements?

Most undergraduate students must maintain a cumulative GPA of 1.85 to 2.0 to remain in good academic standing at the University of Toronto.

What is the U of T GPA Scale Calculator?

The U of T GPA Scale Calculator automatically converts grades into GPA using the official 4.0 scale, helping students track academic progress accurately.

What is U of T GPA Conversion?

U of T GPA Conversion allows you to convert between percentage grades, letter grades, and GPA values according to the university’s 4.0 scale.

What is shown in the U of T GPA Chart?

The U of T GPA Chart displays the relationship between percentage ranges, letter grades, and GPA points — offering a clear visual guide for students.

How does the U of T GPA System work?

The U of T GPA System assigns grade points to each letter grade, creating a cumulative GPA that reflects your overall academic achievement.

What is the U of T GPA Average?

The U of T GPA Average is your cumulative GPA (CGPA), showing the overall grade point average for all your completed courses at the university.
