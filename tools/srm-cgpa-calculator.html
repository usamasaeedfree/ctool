FAQ's

What is the SRM GPA Calculator?

The SRM GPA Calculator is an online academic tool that helps SRM University students compute their semester GPA and overall CGPA according to official university regulations.

How can I calculate GPA in SRM University?

Select your regulation year, enter your subject grades and credits, and click “Calculate GPA” to instantly view your semester GPA and cumulative CGPA.

Which SRM regulations are supported?

The calculator supports SRM University’s 2013, 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018 regulations for accurate grade computation.

Is this SRM GPA calculator accurate?

Yes. The calculator uses SRM University’s official grade point system and regulation data to ensure precise GPA and CGPA results.

What’s the difference between GPA and CGPA?

GPA measures your performance in a single semester, while CGPA represents your cumulative academic performance across all semesters.

How to convert SRM GPA to CGPA?

You can use the built-in CGPA converter in this tool or apply the formula: CGPA = (Sum of Semester GPAs × Credits) ÷ Total Credits.
