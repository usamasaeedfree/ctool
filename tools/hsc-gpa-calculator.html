FAQ's

What is the HSC GPA Calculator?

The HSC GPA Calculator is an online tool that helps students in Bangladesh calculate their Grade Point Average (GPA) based on the official HSC grading system. It automatically converts your marks or grades into GPA.

How is HSC GPA calculated in Bangladesh?

HSC GPA is calculated by converting marks into grade points for each subject, then averaging the total points. If a student has a 4th subject, only the points above 2.00 from that subject are added to the total GPA.

What is the official HSC grading system?

The official grading system in Bangladesh assigns grade points from 5.00 (A+) to 0.00 (F), based on marks ranges from 80-100 for A+ down to 0-32 for F.

Can I calculate my GPA with or without the 4th subject?

Yes, the calculator allows you to include or exclude the 4th subject. When included, the grade point above 2.00 from the 4th subject is added to the total GPA.

Is this calculator accurate for all HSC groups?

Yes, it works for all study groups — Science, Commerce, and Humanities — using the same official grading standards from the Bangladesh Education Board.

Can I use this calculator for previous years like 2021, 2022, or 2024?

Yes, you can use the same HSC GPA Calculator for previous years as the grading system has remained consistent across all sessions.
